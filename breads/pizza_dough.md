Excellent simple dough.

Ingredients
===========

* [ ] 1 1/4 cup warm water
* [ ] 2 tbsp sugar
* [ ] 1 1/2 tsp yeast
* [ ] 2 tbsp neutral oil
* [ ] 2 cups flour
* [ ] 1 cup bread flour
* [ ] 1 tsp salt

Preparation
===========
1. Mix water/yeast/sugar and sit for 5 min. Add oil.

2. Add to flour/salt mix. Allow it to rise for an hour.

3. Make pizza and bake at 425.
