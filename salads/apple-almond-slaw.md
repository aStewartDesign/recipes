All around tasty slaw. Pretty much exactly what you're looking for at a bbq.

Ingredients
===========

##### Dressing
* [ ] 1 cup greek yogurt
* [ ] 1/3 cup mayo
* [ ] 1/4 cup honey
* [ ] 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
* [ ] salt & pepper

##### Coleslaw
* [ ] 1 small cabbage, ~5 cups shredded
* [ ] 1/2 small red cabbage, ~2 cups shredded
* [ ] 2 lg carrots, shredded
* [ ] 2 lg apples, shredded
* [ ] 1/2 cup green onions, sliced
* [ ] 3/4 cup slivered almonds
* [ ] 3/4 cup dried cranberries

Preparation
===========
1. Mix all the dressing ingredients in a bowl.
2. One at a time prep slaw ingredients. Add the dressing after the apples to prevent oxidation.
3. After all ingredients are added, cover and cool in fridge for at least an hour before serving.
